One of the most harmful consequences of social media is the rise of cyberbullying, which tends to be more sinister than traditional bullying, given that online records typically live on the Internet for quite a long time and are hard to control. In this article, we present a three-phase algorithm, called BullyNet, for detecting cyberbullies on Twitter social network.
